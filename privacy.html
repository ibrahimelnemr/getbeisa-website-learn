Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

Beisa Learn does not collect, sell, or share personal data. Practice content is processed on your device.

On-Device Processing

AI practice may use Apple Intelligence when available or a downloaded local model. Practice text, speech, and chat history are not sent to Beisa servers.

Network Usage

Network access is used for optional model downloads and App Store system services. The app does not use analytics, ads, or third-party tracking.

Microphone

Microphone access is used for speech practice. Audio is processed by system speech services or local models and is not collected by Beisa.
